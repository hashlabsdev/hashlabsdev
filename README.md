## 🎯 Current Focus

<div align="center">

### 💊 Pharmacy POS System

</div>

We are actively developing a **full-featured Pharmacy Management & POS System** — built for speed, compliance, and real pharmacy workflows.

**What's inside:**

- 📦 Inventory & stock expiry tracking
- 💰 Billing, invoicing & receipt printing
- 🔄 Supplier & purchase order management
- 📊 Sales analytics & reporting dashboard
- 👤 Customer profiles & purchase history
- 🔐 Role-based access control

> Built on **Laravel + Next.js + PostgreSQL** — production-grade, not a prototype.
